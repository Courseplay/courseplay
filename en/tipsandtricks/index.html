Tips and Tricks

How to create a good working course for baling

    If you do a normal course with start on headland, sooner or later, bales will stay in your way when your courseplay driver tries to turn into the next row.

    The best way to prevent this if you start on your up/down rows.

    That way, there will be no bales on your headland.
